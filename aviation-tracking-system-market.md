<p><p>The aviation tracking system market encompasses several key technologies, including ADS-B (Automatic Dependent Surveillance–Broadcast), FANS (Future Air Navigation System), and PFTS (Performance-Based Flight Tracking System). ADS-B enhances situational awareness by providing real-time aircraft location data to air traffic controllers and nearby aircraft. FANS integrates satellite communication for efficient air traffic management over oceanic and remote areas. PFTS focuses on improving safety and efficiency through advanced tracking and performance monitoring of flights, ensuring optimal route management.</p></p>
