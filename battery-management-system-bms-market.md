<p><ul><li>Distributed</li><li>Centralized</li><li>Modular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>A Battery Management System (BMS) is crucial for monitoring and controlling battery performance, ensuring safety, and optimizing lifespan. The market can be categorized into three types: </p><p>1. **Distributed BMS**: Each cell is monitored independently, offering high reliability and resilience. </p><p>2. **Centralized BMS**: Central control manages multiple cells, simplifying design but potentially increasing failure risks. </p><p>3. **Modular BMS**: Combines aspects of both, allowing flexibility and scalability, making it suitable for various applications from electric vehicles to renewable energy storage systems.</p></p>
