<p><p>The Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) sensor market is primarily divided into Resonators BAW and Delay Lines BAW. Resonators BAW are used for frequency stabilization and filtering in applications such as telecommunications and consumer electronics, utilizing the resonance of acoustic waves. Delay Lines BAW, on the other hand, function by delaying signals through acoustic wave propagation, which is essential in signal processing and high-frequency communication systems. Both types leverage acoustic wave properties to enhance performance in various technological applications.</p></p>
