<p><p>The Carbon Wovens market primarily features two distinct types of weaves: General Weave and Twill Weave. General Weave, characterized by a simple crisscross pattern, provides uniform strength and flexibility, making it suitable for various applications. In contrast, Twill Weave features a zigzag pattern that enhances durability and resistance to fraying. This weave type is often preferred for products requiring superior strength and aesthetic appeal. Both types cater to diverse industries, including aerospace, automotive, and sports equipment.</p></p>
