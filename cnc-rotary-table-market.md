<p><p>CNC rotary tables are categorized into three main types: vertical, horizontal, and tilting. Vertical rotary tables primarily handle workpieces with a vertical orientation, offering ease of loading and unloading. Horizontal rotary tables are designed for horizontal machining, providing stability and precision for heavier workpieces. Tilting rotary tables combine features of both, allowing for angular adjustments, which enhances versatility in complex machining operations. Each type is suited to specific applications, improving efficiency and accuracy in CNC machining processes.</p></p>
