<p><strong>The Dental Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extraoral Imaging</li><li>Intraoral Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental imaging market is primarily divided into two types: extraoral and intraoral imaging. Extraoral imaging involves capturing images from outside the mouth, using techniques such as panoramic X-rays and cephalometric films, offering a broader view of dental structures and aiding in diagnosis. Intraoral imaging, on the other hand, involves capturing images from inside the mouth, including digital X-rays and intraoral cameras, providing detailed visuals for individual teeth and surrounding tissues, facilitating targeted treatment planning and monitoring.</p></p>
