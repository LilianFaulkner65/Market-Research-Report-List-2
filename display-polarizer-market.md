<p><strong>The Display Polarizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TFT Type</li><li>TN Type</li><li>STN Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the display polarizer market, the primary types include TFT (Thin Film Transistor), TN (Twisted Nematic), and STN (Super Twisted Nematic). TFT polarizers are favored for their high image quality and electrical performance, commonly used in advanced screens like smartphones and tablets. TN polarizers offer faster response times at a lower cost but have limited viewing angles and color reproduction. STN polarizers provide improved viewing angles and color depth compared to TN, making them suitable for specific applications like calculators and industrial displays.</p></p>
