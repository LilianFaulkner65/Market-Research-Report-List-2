<p><p>The e-prescribing market comprises two main types: stand-alone systems and integrated systems. Stand-alone systems function independently, allowing healthcare providers to create, send, and manage prescriptions without integration with other health IT solutions. In contrast, integrated systems are part of larger electronic health record (EHR) platforms, offering seamless connectivity between prescribing, patient records, and pharmacy management. This integration enhances workflow efficiency, improves patient safety, and facilitates real-time access to patient data, ultimately streamlining the medication management process.</p></p>
