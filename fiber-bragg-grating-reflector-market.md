<p><p>The Fiber Bragg Grating (FBG) reflector market is primarily segmented into single mode fiber and double-clad fiber. Single mode fibers feature a single light path, enabling high precision and minimal signal loss, making them ideal for telecommunications and sensing applications. In contrast, double-clad fibers incorporate an additional outer cladding layer that allows for enhanced light coupling and increased power handling, making them suitable for high-power laser applications and specialized sensors. Both types cater to diverse technological needs across multiple industries.</p></p>
