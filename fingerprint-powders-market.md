<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Fingerprint Powders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional (Colorant and Base)</li><li>Magnetic (Colorant and Iron Shavings)</li><li>Fluorescent (Light Stimulated Colorant and Base)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fingerprint powders market is categorized into three main types. Conventional powders consist of colorants mixed with a base material, offering a simple approach for revealing fingerprints on various surfaces. Magnetic powders, incorporating fine iron shavings and colorants, utilize magnetism to enhance application and remove excess powder easily. Fluorescent powders utilize light-stimulated colorants, which emit bright hues under specific lighting, facilitating fingerprint detection on dark or complex surfaces. Each type serves distinct purposes in forensic science and crime scene investigation.</p></p>
