<p><p>The fluorite pellets market is primarily categorized into two types: acid grade and metallurgical grade. Acid grade fluorite pellets, with high purity levels, are mainly used in the production of hydrofluoric acid and other fluorine-based chemicals, making them vital for industries like pharmaceuticals and electronics. Both types serve specialized industrial applications.</p></p>
