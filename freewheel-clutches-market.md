<p><p>The Freewheel Clutches market is categorized into three main types: Sprag Type, Roller Type, and Ball Bearing Type. Sprag Type clutches utilize sprags to engage and disengage rotational motion, offering high torque capacity and efficiency. Roller Type clutches employ cylindrical rollers for smoother operation and are often used in automotive applications. Ball Bearing Type clutches feature spherical balls that allow free motion in one direction, providing low friction and reliable performance. Each type serves unique applications across various industries.</p></p>
