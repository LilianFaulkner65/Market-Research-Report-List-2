<p><p>Freightage insurance encompasses three primary market types: ocean shipping, land transportation, and air transport. Ocean shipping covers risks associated with maritime cargo transit, protecting against damage or loss during long voyages. Land transportation insurance safeguards goods transported via trucks or trains, addressing risks such as theft or accidents along highways and railways. Air transport insurance offers coverage for high-value cargo shipped by air, mitigating risks during quick transit times, which often involve exposure to various environmental and handling hazards.</p></p>
