<p><p>The impulse turbine market is characterized by turbines that harness kinetic energy from high-speed jet streams of water, converting it into mechanical energy. They operate effectively in high-head applications. Conversely, counterattack turbines, or reaction turbines, utilize flowing water to produce power through pressure differences, making them suitable for low to medium head ranges. Both types cater to diverse energy generation needs, with impulse turbines favored for their efficiency in specific conditions and counterattack turbines valued for their adaptability in varying water flow scenarios.</p></p>
