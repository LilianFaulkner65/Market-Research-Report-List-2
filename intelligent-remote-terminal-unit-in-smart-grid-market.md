Wired RTUs utilize fixed communication lines for stable and secure data transmission, making them ideal for urban environments with high infrastructure availability. In contrast, Wireless RTUs leverage cellular, satellite, or radio technologies, providing flexibility and scalability, especially in rural or remote areas where traditional wiring may be challenging or cost-prohibitive.</p></p>
