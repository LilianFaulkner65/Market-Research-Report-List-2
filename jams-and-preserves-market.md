<p><p>Jams and preserves are sweet spreads made from fruits, sugar, and sometimes additional ingredients like pectin or spices. Jams are typically made from crushed fruit and cooked until thickened, while preserves contain larger pieces of fruit, offering a chunkier texture. Health-conscious consumers are gravitating towards options that contain fewer preservatives and artificial additives, prompting manufacturers to innovate with healthier recipes.
