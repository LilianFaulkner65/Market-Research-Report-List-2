<p><strong>The Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) Geomembrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Layer Geomembranes</li><li>Double Layer Geomembranes</li><li>Three Layer Geomembranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) geomembrane market is categorized into three types based on layer composition. Single layer geomembranes consist of a single sheet of LDPE, offering basic impermeability. Double layer geomembranes feature two LDPE layers, providing enhanced strength and durability for applications such as landfill liners and water containment. Three layer geomembranes include an additional layer, often a different polymer, to further improve performance characteristics like puncture resistance, making them suitable for more demanding environments.</p></p>
