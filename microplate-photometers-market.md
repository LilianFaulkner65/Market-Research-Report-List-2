<p><strong>The Microplate Photometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Filter Microplate Reader</li><li>Optical Grating Microplate Reader</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microplate photometers are essential for analyzing samples in high-throughput screening and various assays. The market is divided into two main types: optical filter microplate readers and optical grating microplate readers. Optical filter microplate readers utilize specific wavelength filters to isolate light for measuring absorbance, fluorescence, or luminescence, providing straightforward and cost-effective results. In contrast, optical grating microplate readers employ diffraction gratings to disperse light across a spectrum, enabling more versatile and precise measurements across a broader wavelength range.</p></p>
