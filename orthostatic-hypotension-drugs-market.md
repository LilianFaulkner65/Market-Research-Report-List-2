<p><p>The Orthostatic Hypotension Drugs market includes several key types of medications. Midodrine is an alpha-agonist that increases blood pressure by constricting blood vessels. Northera (droxidopa) is a prodrug that converts to norepinephrine, enhancing vascular tone and blood flow. Fludrocortisone, a mineralocorticoid, helps retain sodium and water, thus increasing blood volume. Additionally, other drugs in this market encompass various agents that aim to alleviate symptoms of orthostatic hypotension, contributing to diverse treatment options for affected patients.</p></p>
