<p><p>The ostomy devices market consists of three primary types: colostomy, ileostomy, and urostomy drainage bags. Colostomy drainage bags are used for waste management in patients with colostomies, allowing for the collection of stool from the colon. Ileostomy drainage bags serve similar functions but are designed for those who have undergone ileostomy procedures, managing waste from the small intestine. Urostomy drainage bags cater to individuals with urinary diversions, collecting urine when the bladder is bypassed. Each type addresses specific medical needs and improves patient quality of life.</p></p>
