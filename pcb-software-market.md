<p><p>The PCB software market can be categorized into two primary types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises software is installed locally on a user's hardware, offering full control over data and customization, but requiring significant IT resources for maintenance and updates. In contrast, cloud-based PCB software is hosted on remote servers, enabling easy access from anywhere and reducing the need for extensive IT support. This model typically offers subscription pricing, scalability, and automatic updates, appealing to organizations seeking flexibility and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
