<p><strong>The Self Contained Breathing Apparatus Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-Circuit SCBA</li><li>Closed-Circuit SCBA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Self Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA) market is primarily divided into two types: Open-Circuit SCBA and Closed-Circuit SCBA. Open-Circuit SCBA releases exhaled air into the environment, making it suitable for short-duration use, mainly in firefighting and rescue operations. In contrast, Closed-Circuit SCBA recycles exhaled air, removing carbon dioxide and adding oxygen, allowing for longer operational durations, ideal for industrial applications and underwater activities. Both types ensure respiratory protection in hazardous environments, catering to diverse user needs.</p></p>
