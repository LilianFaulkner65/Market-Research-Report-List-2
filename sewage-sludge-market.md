<p><ul><li>Raw primary sludge</li><li>Secondary sludge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sewage sludge market primarily consists of two types: raw primary sludge and secondary sludge. Raw primary sludge is generated from the initial sedimentation process in wastewater treatment, containing a high organic matter content. It is often used for energy recovery or as a fertilizer. Secondary sludge, on the other hand, is produced from the biological treatment of wastewater, rich in microorganisms. It is typically treated further for biogas production or composting, playing a vital role in sustainable waste management practices.</p></p>
