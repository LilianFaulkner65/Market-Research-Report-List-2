<p><p>The tire market is primarily divided into radial and bias tires. Radial tires feature layers of fabric arranged radially, offering better fuel efficiency, handling, and tread wear, making them popular for passenger vehicles. Bias tires, on the other hand, have layers crisscrossed at angles, providing sturdiness and sidewall flexibility, which is beneficial for certain applications like trucks and off-road vehicles. Each type serves different performance needs and preferences, influencing market demand and consumer choice in the automotive industry.</p></p>
