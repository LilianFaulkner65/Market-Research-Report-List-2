<p><p>The truck steering system market encompasses three main types: Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS), which uses hydraulic fluid to aid steering, enhancing control and reducing driver effort; Electric Power Steering (EPS), which relies on electric motors for steering assistance, offering improved fuel efficiency and responsiveness; and Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS), a hybrid system combining both hydraulic and electric components, providing enhanced adaptability and performance. Each type serves distinct operational requirements and technological advancements in commercial vehicles.</p></p>
