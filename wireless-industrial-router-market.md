<p><ul><li>Wall-mount</li><li>Embedded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless industrial router market consists of two primary types: wall-mount and embedded routers. Wall-mount routers are designed for easy installation on walls, providing robust wireless connectivity for larger industrial environments. They typically offer advanced features like multiple Ethernet ports and enhanced signal range. Embedded routers, on the other hand, are integrated within devices or machinery, offering seamless connectivity in compact spaces. Both types serve critical roles in supporting industrial automation, data transmission, and remote monitoring applications.</p></p>
